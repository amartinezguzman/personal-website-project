Personal Website Project: Milestone 1


Purpose:This personal website project will provide a clear and concise overview of my professional skills focused on web development, coaching, community and education.


Audience: This website is designed with a professional audience in mind, and is focused on targeting web development communities, non-profit organizations and financial institutions nationwide. Target audience includes: potential customers, community & outreach specialists, recruiters, hiring managers and professional peers.


Goal:The goal of my website is to create new freelancing, collaboration and employment opportunities and grow my network by showcasing and promoting my work.


Persona

Name:Emma Carbajal


Age:34


Profession:Founder of Happy Feet Dance Studio, a newly established business. They are looking to build their online presence by creating a website that allows them to display upcoming events, their dance schedule and manage class registrations.


Technology:Apple 21.5” iMac (macOS Big Sur, version 11.0.1 operating system), iPhone 12 Pro 5G speed unlimited data (iOS14.7 operating system). As a proficient user of technology, Emma possess basic fundamental computer skills, is comfortable performing tasks such as creating and managing files, and can navigate through the internet with ease.


Attitudes and Behaviors:Creative, enjoys meeting new people and spreading her love of dance. Emma is motivated to grow her business. She is business-minded, and values professionalism. Emma prefers to spend time developing ideas and then execute it, rather than just jumping in. Emma favors user friendly websites that allow for varying types of users to access her services.


Frustrations and Needs:Emma is frustrated by excessive, wordy and unclear information. She is uninspired by lack of originality. Emma will not hire someone that has an unprofessional online presence. She dislikes having to micromanage people and dealing with difficult personalities. She is looking for an experienced candidate that can work well in teams and be able to work with minimal supervision.


Goals:Hire a strong candidate that can turn her vision into a functional and easy to use website that will attract new customers.


User Story:As a user, I need to be able to quickly learn about a candidate’s experience and skills to determine if they are a good fit.
