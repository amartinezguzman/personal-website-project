Personal Website Project:

Milestone 2

Web's Design: The mobile-first design will be simple, cohesive and visually engaging. This will include only displaying necessary text. A user will be able to navigate this site with ease and quickly learn about who I am. Some of the featured content will only be available in the desktop version due to spacing and overall design.

Navigation: This section will feature my name and a centered navbar that takes users to their desired section within the webpage. It will also have a picture (not yet selected) right below that extends across the webpage. The main goal of the picture will be to support desired branding throughout and yield visual interest.

About Me: This section will provide a professional picture of myself and a brief introduction with a welcome message encouraging collaboration. In addition, it will display icons directing users to my GitHub & LinkedIn.

Skills: This section will provide detailed information highlighting three main components regarding my professional and academic skills. All skills will be accompanied by a relevant picture.

  • Web development will include lists of skills learned in bootcamp and links to capstone & future projects.

  • Education & coaching will provide overview of relevant experience.

  • Community will highlight my work in social justice and community service.

Gallery: This section will provide a personal touch and provide a user with additional information about my interests and hobbies. This will be achieved by displaying pictures that are important to me. The mobile version will showcase pictures in a carousel.

Contact: This section will contain a styled contact form with the option to include a message.

Footer: This section is designed for styling only.
